Scope of FIFA and its impact on soccer

Our domain surrounds data pulled from FIFA video games. FIFA is one of the most popular video games on the market, attracting over 10 million players worldwide on PC and console. Soccer/football is also the world’s most popular sport, with 1.1 billion viewers tuning into the 2018 World Cup final over the course of the match. Soccer plays a huge role in world culture, drives local economies in some cities, and brings people together. It’s important to study data surrounding trends and patterns in an industry that has such an impact on world culture. 

Kaggle Dataset

We are using a Kaggle dataset by user Stefano Leone that has FIFA data from 2015 to 2020. It has about 104 columns of attributes about each player’s skill sets, wages, their positions, and where they are from. It includes information for over 100 players, and because it includes data from the past five years, it will be possible to see trends over time. 

The information about each player is very specific, giving objective statistics about their sprint speed, tackling abilities, and even their mental ability to score penalties. There is also general information about each player such as their preferred position, the club they play for, and the country they are from. The data is free use, and includes images of the players as well.

Nationality vs Average Overall

This first visual shows the Top 20 countries with the most players loaded in FIFA 2020. When hovering on each bar, the user can see the exact number of players and the country’s average player rating. The bars are visually encoded by continent, so its clear to see which countries dominate the footballing world. On the top right, we added a filter to control distribution by continent. 

Age vs Overall

This second visual aims to show if there is a correlation between the age of the player and what their overall rating in FIFA is. When hovering over each data point, the user can see the player’s name. The orange colors represent players that are under the age of 30 and the turquoise color represents players that are over the age of 30.
